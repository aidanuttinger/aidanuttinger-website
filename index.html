Our Manifesto

Business should be a force for good.

Somewhere along the way, the relationship between businesses and the agencies that serve them got badly broken. Agencies started optimising for their own revenue instead of their clients' results. Retainers kept flowing whether the results came or not.

We started Snyper because we believe there's a better way. A model where the agency only wins when the client wins. Where every dollar spent is tied to a measurable outcome. Where technology serves people — not the other way around.

The old model is broken

  • Monthly retainers with vague "brand awareness" metrics
  • Thousands spent on ads with no clear link to revenue
  • Reports full of vanity numbers that don't pay your bills
  • Agencies that profit whether you grow or not
  • Your best leads sitting untouched in a CRM gathering dust

What we believe in

  • You should only pay when you see results
  • Every dollar should be tied to a measurable return
  • AI should remove grunt work, not replace human connection
  • Your existing database is a goldmine most agencies ignore
  • Business growth and doing good are not mutually exclusive
